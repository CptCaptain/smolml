<Callout variant="insight" title="how to read this">
The one metric is **validation bits-per-byte (bpb) at a fixed total-FLOP budget** on a tiny
corpus. "Faster" means **lower bpb at equal FLOPs** &mdash; not wall-clock, not parameter count,
not "it answered a question." Everything here is in service of reading that one curve honestly.
</Callout>
